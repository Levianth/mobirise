Who Am I?

My name is Hannah Svraka. I'm 17 years old. I graduated with a diploma from Richard Gahr High school in June 2018. And I am currently attending Cerritos College since August 2018. A few hobbies of mine are creating new ideas with Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. With my free time, I enjoy playing games, listening to music, and browsing Reddit.
